To begin with "Phonetics" (/fəˈnɛtɪks/) is the study and classification of speech sounds. Given below is a list of 'phonemes' collected from the Google Dictionary:

eɪ: sound of A /eɪ/ (as in bay /beɪ/, aberration /ˌabəˈreɪʃ(ə)n/) (Note: you will not find 'e' alone in any pronunciation)
ʌɪ: sound of I /ʌɪ/ (as in eye /ʌɪ/)
əʊ: sound of O /əʊ/ (sounds like the alphabet O, as in bogus /ˈbəʊɡəs/)
aʊ: as in douse /daʊs/, out /aʊt/

iː: sound of E /iː/ (as in eel /iːl/, east /iːst/)
ɑː: aah (as in raja /ˈrɑːdʒɑː/, reprimand /ˈrɛprɪmɑːnd/)
əː: as in birth /bəːθ/, subvert /səbˈvəːt/
ɔː: as in or /ɔː/, exorbitant /ɪɡˈzɔːbɪt(ə)nt/
uː: ooh /uː/ (as in U /juː/)

ə: uh (as in guttural /ˈɡʌt(ə)r(ə)l/)
	ʌ: uh (as in up /ʌp/, guttural /ˈɡʌt(ə)r(ə)l/)
	ʊ: uh (as in 'erudition' /ˌɛrʊˈdɪʃ(ə)n/)
ɛ: eh (as in 'erudition' /ˌɛrʊˈdɪʃ(ə)n/)
ɪ: as in if /ɪf/, is /ɪz/, 'erudition' /ˌɛrʊˈdɪʃ(ə)n/
	i: as in viability /vʌɪəˈbɪlɪti/
ɒ: aw (as in dog /dɒɡ/, absolve /əbˈzɒlv/, docker /ˈdɒkə/)

a: (as in admonition /ˌadməˈnɪʃ(ə)n/, as /az/, bad /bad/, and /and/)
θ: th (as in birth /bəːθ/)
ŋ: as in swing /swɪŋ/, perfunctory /pəˈfʌŋ(k)t(ə)ri/

j: sound of Y (as in yes /jɛs/, you /juː/)
ʃ: sound of 'sh' as in sunshine /ˈsʌnʃʌɪn/
dʒ: j (as in jug /dʒʌɡ/, raja /ˈrɑːdʒɑː/)
